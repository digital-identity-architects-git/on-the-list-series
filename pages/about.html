About the Author

Eric Brister

Eric Brister writes contemporary novels about ambition and the quiet machinery of who gets to belong. On the List is his debut series — a story that began with a single image: a door, at the back of a place that mattered, with no handle on the outside.

The series

The books follow Mara Vance from the night her name is read aloud off a list she never asked to be on, through everything that name costs her and everything it makes possible. The series is written to be read in order, beginning with Book One.

Start by flipping through Chapter One, or get in touch.
