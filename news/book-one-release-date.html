On the List: Book One Sets Its Release Date

By Eric Brister · May 30, 2026

On the List: Book One will publish on October 6, 2026. Today the series also reveals the cover and opens a free preview of the opening chapter.

"This is the night the whole series turns on," said author Eric Brister. "If you only read one chapter to know whether the series is for you, read this one."

Readers can flip through Chapter One now, and sign up for pre-order updates to be notified the moment the book is available.
