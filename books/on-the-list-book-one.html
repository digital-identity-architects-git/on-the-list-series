On the List

A novel by Eric Brister · Releasing October 6, 2026

Mara Vance spent five years learning what it costs to keep her name on the list — the favors, the silence, the door at the back of the Meridian that only opens from the inside. The night it finally swings wide, she learns the list was never about getting in. It was about who they decide to leave out, and what they expect from the ones they call.
